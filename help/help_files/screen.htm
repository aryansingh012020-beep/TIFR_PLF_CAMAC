SCREEN SELECTOR

The display is organised into a number of screens. Each screen can hold upto 12 spectra (1d and 2d). The first 12 spectra will appear on Screen 1, the next 12 will be on Screen 2 and so on. The 1d spectra come first and then the 2d spectra. You can display any of the first 4 screens by clicking 1 to 4 from the screen selector. To go to screen numbers beyond that (when you have more than 48 spectra) you can use + (next screen), - (previous screen), Last (last screen) or N (select an arbitary screen).
